What is Synthetic Data?

Synthetic data is artificially generated data that mimics real data in terms of statistical properties and patterns. It is used for testing and training purposes in various industries, including data science and artificial intelligence.

Learn More

Why Use Synthetic Data?

Data Privacy

Synthetic data eliminates the risk of exposing sensitive information, as it is not real data. This is especially important for industries that deal with sensitive data, such as healthcare and finance.

Cost-Effective

Generating synthetic data is much more cost-effective than collecting and cleaning real data. It also eliminates the need for data storage and maintenance costs.

Customizable

Synthetic data can be tailored to specific needs and scenarios, making it a valuable tool for testing and training in various industries.

Scalable

With synthetic data, there are no limitations on the amount of data that can be generated, making it a scalable solution for businesses of all sizes.
