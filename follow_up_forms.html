What is the Competitive Analysis Tool?

The Competitive Analysis Tool is a process that collects information from users through a series of questions and then provides them with a competitive analysis of their top five competitors. The analysis is presented in chart form and users are asked to provide their knowledge about each competitor. Users are also given the option to request a free half hour consultation.

How does it work?

Users will be asked a series of questions about their business and their top five competitors. The tool will then gather and analyze the data to provide a comprehensive chart comparing the user's business to their competitors. Users can also request a free half hour consultation to discuss the results and gain further insights.

Why use the Competitive Analysis Tool?

The Competitive Analysis Tool provides valuable insights into a user's competitors and their own business. This information can help businesses make informed decisions and improve their strategies to stay ahead of the competition. The tool is also user-friendly and provides quick and accurate results.
